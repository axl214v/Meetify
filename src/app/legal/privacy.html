Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Meetify is an independent project maintained by a single developer. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it — in plain language.

What we collect

DataWhy
Email addressAccount creation and login
Password hash (bcrypt)Authentication — your plaintext password is never stored
UsernameDisplay name inside the app
Avatar imageOptional, only if you upload one

We do not collect IP address logs, browser fingerprints, analytics events, or any data beyond what is listed above.

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to operate your account. We do not sell, share, rent, or otherwise disclose your data to any third party — ever.

Where data is stored

All data is stored on a server located in Switzerland. Switzerland operates under the Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP), which provides protections equivalent to or exceeding the EU GDPR.

🇨🇭 Swiss-hosted · Strong data protection laws · No third-party data sharing

How long we keep your data

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all associated data — email, password hash, username, avatar — is permanently and irreversibly removed from our database.

Cookies

We use a single session cookie (token) strictly required for authentication. No tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies are used.

Your rights

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correct — update inaccurate data via your profile settings
  • Delete — remove your account and all data via profile settings, or by emailing us

Email privacy@meetify.cc for any of these requests. We respond within 30 days.

Security

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (cost factor 10) — never stored in plaintext
  • All connections use HTTPS
  • Authentication uses short-lived JWT tokens in httpOnly cookies

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@meetify.cc
